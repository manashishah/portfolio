About Me

As a UI/UX designer, I have a strong foundation in crafting engaging and user-friendly digital experiences. My journey in design began with a Bachelor of Information Management from St. Xavier's College in Nepal in 2018. However, my passion for creating seamless and visually appealing interfaces led me to further my education. I recently completed a Postgraduate Certificate in Digital Product Design at Sheridan College in Oakville, Canada, in 2023, where I honed my skills and embraced cutting-edge design principles. Currently, I'm pursuing Web Development and Front-End Design at George Brown College in Toronto, seeking to bridge the gap between design and development. With three years of hands-on experience as a Junior Front-End Developer and Designer, I am committed to delivering innovative solutions.

Outside of work, I find solace in the world of music & books and express my creativity through the intricate art of mandala drawing.
